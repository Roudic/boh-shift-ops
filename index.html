BOH Shift Ops

Schedule the week. Set the floor. Own the rush.

The back-of-house board for restaurant teams — week schedule, station setup, and a personal week for every teammate.

Open the app See how it works

Why it exists

Paper boards don’t keep up with a real BOH week.

When the schedule and the floor plan live in different places, rushes get messy. BOH Shift Ops keeps both in one place.

One boardfor the week and the floor

Leaders build the week, publish each period setup, and teammates open My Week to see their hours, leadership, and stations.

How it works

Three moves that run the week.

Built for shift leaders who need clarity before the doors open.

  1. 01

    Build the week

    Paint shifts, set period blocks, and assign Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner leaders on one schedule.

  2. 02

    Set the floor

    Drop people onto stations for each rush, publish when the setup is ready, and print a gameplan that fits the sheet.

  3. 03

    Share the week

    Every login links to a roster name — My Week shows their schedule, leadership windows, and posted positions.

Access

The right tools for each role.

Admin controls who can sign in. Managers run the board. Team members see their week.

Admin

Full control — users, roles, publish status, and every board in the store.

Manager

Schedule, shift setup, skills, leaders, and publish when a period is ready.

Team member

My Week only — linked to their roster name for hours, leadership, and stations.
